M&M Sorter

Welcome to my GIP project.

First, we had to come up with 3 ideas.

My 3 ideas were a trading bot, a company making online or a drone.

Eventually none of those 3 ideas came to fruition. Mr. Gilles suggested that I make an M&M sorter.

The idea is simple, just use an arduino, servo motor, and a color sensor to make an M&M sorter.

Throughout this page, I will teach you how to make an M&M sorter.
